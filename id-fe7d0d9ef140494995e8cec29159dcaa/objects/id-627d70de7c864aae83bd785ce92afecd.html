The Baseline Overview diagram describes the high level business concepts and processes for statistical data dissemination. There are two main processes - Data Dissemination and Data Search. Statistical data is made available on the National Statisticial Organizations' websites in a structured form (SDMX, PX) or as PDF files. This service is accessible using a parametrized HTTP request. Source: https://www.statcan.gc.ca/en/developers/sdmx/user-guide Example: 1.
